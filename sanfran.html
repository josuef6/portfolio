San Francisco

During my last year in undergrad I was given the chance to travel to the Bay Area to watch the College Football National Championship game. I was awarded a free student ticket through Taco Bell to support my school, Clemson University. It was a great experience and I was able to watch my school win the National Championship live!

While I was there I was able to explore San Francisco and visit lots of landmarks popular within the city. There was even enough time for me to visit a winery in Napa Valley.
